What tools will I need to pay for?

Our systems are built on best-in-class tools. You will need your own paid subscriptions for Notion (Plus plan or higher) and Zapier (Starter plan or higher), depending on your team size and usage.

What do you need from me to get started?

The most important input is your time during the 2-hour Discovery & Mapping session. You'll need to be ready to walk us through your current processes. After that, we just need access to the relevant accounts, and we handle the rest.

What happens during the 30-day support period?

This is your success guarantee. For 30 days after launch, you have direct Slack/email access to us for any questions, bug fixes, or minor tweaks to the system. We're on standby to ensure your team's adoption is smooth and successful.
